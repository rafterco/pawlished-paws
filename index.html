A Different Kind of Grooming Experience

At Pawlished Paws, we believe grooming should be a calm, enjoyable experience for every dog. Based in Carrowhubbuck South, Enniscrone, we've created a quiet, unhurried studio where your dog is treated with patience and care from start to finish.

No other dogs. No distractions. Just you, your pup, and a groomer who genuinely loves what they do.
